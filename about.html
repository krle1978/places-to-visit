About Places To Visit

Places To Visit is a travel project where I combine photography, storytelling, architecture, culture and seasonal recommendations — showing destinations through the eyes of someone who truly explored them.

My Story & Mission

I love traveling, observing people, walking through old cities, exploring architecture and understanding the culture behind every corner. Over time, I realized that my journeys were more than just trips — they were collections of stories, moments, and insights.

This website was born to document those experiences, and more importantly — to help others discover places in a meaningful and practical way. Not only “what to see”, but also “when to visit”, “how it feels”, and “what makes the place special”.

How This Project Works

  • 1. Travel
    I visit cities, historical places, cultural sites, and natural landscapes.
  • 2. Observe & Photograph
    I capture architecture, daily life, hidden corners and small moments.
  • 3. Research & Learn
    I explore the history, traditions, and the story behind each location.
  • 4. Write the Story
    I describe the place in a human way — not just as a tourist guide, but as a lived experience.
  • 5. Recommend by Seasons
    I suggest the best time to visit: spring, summer, autumn, or winter.
