Alba Iulia: The Heart of Transylvanian History

Visited in October 2025 • Best season: Autumn

Alba Iulia is one of the most symbolic cities in Romania — a monumental citadel, Roman ruins, elegant baroque gates and wide fortress walls create a timeless walking experience.

What I Experienced

Alba Iulia instantly feels grand. The fortress gates are impressive, the interior is clean and beautifully restored, and the atmosphere is slow, calm and monumental. Walking its huge star-shaped citadel feels like stepping through centuries of layered history.

Baroque gates, Roman ruins, museums and a long pedestrian spine make this one of the best-designed historical cities in Eastern Europe.
