Salina Turda: Romania’s Underground Cathedral of Salt

Visited in October 2025 • Best season: Autumn

Salina Turda is not just a salt mine — it's one of the most surreal underground places in Europe. Vast chambers carved entirely from salt, a bright underground lake, curved wooden structures and echoing silence make this place feel like another world.

What I Experienced

The descent into Salina Turda feels like entering a sci-fi movie. As the elevator opens, you see enormous salt chambers illuminated by futuristic lights. The air is cool, incredibly clean and slightly salty — people come here even for therapy sessions.

The underground lake with wooden boats looks almost unreal. It’s a place that stays in your memory long after you leave.
